TORCAMP Chemical Engineering: College Life, Career Prospects, and Soft Skills

TORCAMP 2021 is a bootcamp for chemical engineering freshmen to kickstart their studies in chemical engineering major. The event gives insights to chemical engineering freshmen how to start their journey as future process engineers, what to expect, and which soft skills are needed for the career. This event is held in two days, 30th – 31st July 2021 virtually via Google Meet platform, and attended by more than 20 students from more than 10 universities from around Indonesia.

Date

30th -31st July 2021

Time

13.00 – 15.00 WIB

Pricing

About the Speakers

Muhammad Yusuf Arya Ramadhan, S.T., M.T.

Arya is a lecturer, researcher, and co-founder of Torche Education and Kopi Nyantai. He graduated from Universitas Indonesia with master’s degree majoring in Chemical Engineering. As a researcher, he has published numerous scopus indexed journals in bioprocess engineering topics.


Felix Pratama, S.T.

Felix is a Senior Distribution Process Improvement (PDCA) at PT Sayap Mas Utama (known as Wings Group). He graduated from Universitas Indonesia in bachelor’s degree majoring in chemical engineering with Cumulative GPA of 3,9. As a undergraduate student, he received some awards, including Mahasiswa Berprestasi Akademis from the Department of Chemical Engineering Universitas Indonesia.
